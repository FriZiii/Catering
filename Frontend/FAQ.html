FAQ

A boxed meal diet, also known as a meal delivery diet or a meal plan service, is a dietary approach where pre-portioned and balanced meals are delivered directly to the individual's doorstep. The meals are conveniently packaged in individual containers or boxes, ready to be consumed without the need for extensive meal preparation or cooking.

We offer a wide variety of meals to cater to different tastes, preferences, and dietary needs. You can choose your diet and a number of calories you need and we will prepare tasty and unique meals.

The delivery hours for the diets are from 2:00 AM to 9:00 AM (day of order fulfillment).

The most preferred method of delivery for us is to hand over the diet package directly to the customer. Sets that are delivered during the late-night/early-morning hours are left at the specified address outside the door.

The box catering service accommodates a range of dietary restrictions and preferences. This includes options for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other specific dietary needs. The service strives to provide diverse and customizable meal choices to cater to various dietary requirements and personal preferences.
